# Lore — the seedbed

Parked story-seeds: kernels of myth or mood mapped onto the engine's occlusion
law, held until one can name its own finish line. Nothing here is decided; nothing
here is content the engine reads yet.

A seed that earns a *mechanic* graduates — to an ideologue, then an `adrqtcc`
entry (see METHOD.md). A seed that earns a *being* becomes a
`res/entities/*.being`. Until then it waits. Ideas wait; they are not judged.

## The seeds

- **titans-the-faded-pantheon** — to be unwatched is to erode to mere lineage.
- **cronus-kept-his-teeth** — the inverse: a thing that feeds on being watched.
- **the-four-pillars** — structure as horror; corners you notice only when one falls.
- **mnemosyne-and-lethe** — the two rivers; the physics of the fade. *(graduated → [`../ideologues/the-liminal-fade.md`](../ideologues/the-liminal-fade.md), with a finish line)*
