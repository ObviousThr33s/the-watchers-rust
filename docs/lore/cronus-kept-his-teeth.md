# Cronus — the one who kept his teeth

*A seed. Parked, not judged — no finish line yet. The mirror of
`titans-the-faded-pantheon.md`.*

## The kernel

Of the brothers, Cronus is the one nobody forgets — and that's the whole point.
He castrated his father, ate his children, and was thrown down by Zeus. The myths
kept every tooth. Where his brothers faded to genealogy (see
`titans-the-faded-pantheon.md`), Cronus stayed sharp *because* he stayed feared.
Memory was his armor.

## The beat

The faded are forgotten into harmlessness. The feared are remembered into power.
Attention is not safety — for some things, attention is food.

## Why it belongs here

This is the dangerous inversion of the occlusion law. The engine's default:
unwatched things move (the dark is where they act). The Titans seed adds a second
case: unwatched things *erode*. Cronus is the third — a being for whom being
watched is not exposure but **feeding**.

Vesh already leans this way: `stalk / feed / recoil`, *"they let you see them."*
Cronus is that taken to its end — an entity that grows on the player's gaze and
starves in the dark, the exact opposite of a faded Titan. Two poles; most beings
live somewhere between them.

## Possible uses (undecided)

- Naming: hold **Cronus** for the apex, not for the faded naming pool.
